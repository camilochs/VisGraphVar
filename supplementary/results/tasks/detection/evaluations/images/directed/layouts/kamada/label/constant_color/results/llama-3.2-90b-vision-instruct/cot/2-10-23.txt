```json
  "total_edges": 19,

```

**Explanation**

*   We started by identifying the nodes, which are the distinct points or circles in the graph. There are 10 nodes in this graph.
*   Next, we identified the edges, which are the lines connecting the nodes. There are 19 edges in this graph.
*   Both the nodes and edges are represented in black.
*   Therefore, the graph consists of 10 nodes and 19 edges, with all nodes and edges represented in black.
